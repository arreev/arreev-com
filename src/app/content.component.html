Free route planning and following solutions.
Easy to use.  No hardware required.  No monthly fees.
 
Arreev allows you to set up a travel path from your start to your destination ... your friends and family can track your progress until you arrive safely at your destination ... your GPS location is sent from your phone or your car's built-in Android-Auto or CarPlay system.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Private and secure - you choose who can subscribe to your routes.
Taking a road trip ? Travel with peace of mind. Set up your route in myArreev, add car(s), invite subscribers. Then your guests can follow your progress until you safely arrive at your destination.
